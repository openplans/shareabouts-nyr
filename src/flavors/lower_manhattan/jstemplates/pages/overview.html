About this map

This map was published in October 2013 to help residents provide input into Lower Manhattan’s Community’s Reconstruction Plan. We have received 67 comments, which will inform the projects and actions for rebuilding and resiliency to be included in the Community Reconstruction Plans.

While we have closed off this map for further comments, you may submit further input to nyrising@hraadvisors.com (include the community’s name in the subject line).

See the initial Concept Plan developed for your community here. Final Reconstruction Plans will be released in April 2014.

If you have comments or questions about the NY Rising Program please email the NY State Office of Storm Recovery at info@stormrecovery.ny.gov

Thank you for your input!
